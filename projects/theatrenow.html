An intuitive theatrical rights acquisition website
Our UX team worked collaboratively on developing a high-fidelity prototype website. Initially creating mockups in AdobeXD such as the one below.

During each sprint we came together and evaluated our current designs before moving on to the next prototype phase.
The image below represents the result of a design meeting and an interface redesign implemented in a high-fidelity prototype.


Over the 20 weeks this project covered, our sponsors presented us with a requirements pivot which changed the basis of our entire design concepts.
We began to redesign and prototype based on the new requirements, as well as create new features.

One feature which contributed to the success of the redesign was a social media content generator.
The main users of this system are high school theater programs, and our website was designed to make marketing their productions seamless.
I designed a simple social media post generator which users could populate with their own data and instantly share across platforms to market their production.

This generator can be used for multiple types of social media post, allowing for consistency with each production, and convenience for users.
What was important for this project was seeing users needs, understanding the sponsor needs and requirements, and going above and beyond when designing.
