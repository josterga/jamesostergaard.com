A ux/ui redesign for the GroupMe messaging platform
The GroupMe platform at the start of this project had a learning curve, built for companies as a mass-messaging platform, the average user had trouble managing and maintaing groups.
This appeared to be a major pain point in the use of GroupMe. Our team followed a fullly documented research and design process to bring GroupMe into the modern age of messaging.

The main redesign components were restructuring and recluttering the user interface for maximum efficiency, and ability to recognize functions.
